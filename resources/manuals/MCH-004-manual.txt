CONVEYOR SYSTEM MODEL CS-400 MAINTENANCE MANUAL

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: CS-400-Packaging
- Rated Speed: 1.5 m/s
- Drive Motor: 3-Phase Induction Motor, 2.2kW
- Max Belt Load: 150 kg
- Normal Temperature Range: 20°C to 55°C
- Maximum Allowable Temperature: 65°C
- Normal Vibration Range: 0.1 to 2.5 mm/s (RMS)
- Normal Current Draw: 4.0A to 5.5A

TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE & FAILURE CODES:
- CODE E101: Motor Overheating
  - Cause: High ambient temperature, fan clog, or drive belt slippage.
  - Action: Clean cooling fan, check drive pulley tension, check lubrication.
- CODE E102: Belt Slippage
  - Cause: Drive pulley wear or low tension.
  - Action: Tension belt, inspect drive pulley.
- CODE E103: Joint Jam
  - Cause: Foreign object or bearing wear.
  - Action: Shutdown immediately, clear joint.

LUBRICATION SCHEDULE:
- Bearings: Every 500 operating hours with Mobilux EP 2.
- Drive Chain: Every 200 operating hours with SAE 30 oil.
