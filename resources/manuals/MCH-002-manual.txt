ROBOTIC WELDER MODEL RW-500 MAINTENANCE MANUAL

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: RW-500-ArcCell
- Machine Type: Robotic Welding Arm
- Welding Type: MIG/MAG Arc Welding
- Axis Count: 6-axis robotic arm
- Payload Capacity: 20 kg
- Normal Servo Temperature Range: 25°C to 58°C
- Maximum Allowable Servo Temperature: 68°C
- Normal Joint Vibration Range: 0.1 to 1.8 mm/s
- Normal Current Draw: 8.0A to 14.0A
- Shielding Gas: CO2 / Argon mix

TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE & FAILURE CODES:
- CODE R301: Joint Servo Overheating
  - Cause: Excessive duty cycle, servo fan blockage, joint friction, or lubrication failure.
  - Action: Reduce duty cycle, clean servo fan, inspect joint lubrication.

- CODE R302: Arc Instability
  - Cause: Wire feed issue, incorrect voltage, poor grounding, or shielding gas flow problem.
  - Action: Check wire feeder, verify voltage settings, inspect grounding clamp, check gas flow.

- CODE R303: Joint Position Error
  - Cause: Encoder error, calibration drift, mechanical obstruction, or worn gear reducer.
  - Action: Recalibrate robot, inspect encoder, clear obstruction, inspect reducer.

- CODE R304: Excessive Joint Vibration
  - Cause: Loose mounting bolts, gear backlash, worn reducer, or unstable base.
  - Action: Tighten mounting bolts, inspect gearbox, check base stability.

MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE:
- Joint Lubrication: Every 500 operating hours.
- Wire Feeder Inspection: Every 100 operating hours.
- Torch Nozzle Cleaning: Every shift.
- Servo Fan Cleaning: Every 250 operating hours.
- Full Calibration: Every 1000 operating hours.

SAFETY NOTES:
- Disable robot motion before entering welding cell.
- Use welding PPE while servicing active welding area.
- Confirm emergency stop and safety gate interlocks before maintenance.
