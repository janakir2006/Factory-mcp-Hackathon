INJECTION MOLDER MODEL IM-330 MAINTENANCE MANUAL

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: IM-330-Plastics
- Machine Type: Plastic Injection Molding Machine
- Clamp Force: 330 tons
- Heater Zones: 5
- Hydraulic Motor: 18.5kW
- Normal Barrel Temperature Range: 180°C to 240°C
- Maximum Allowable Hydraulic Oil Temperature: 60°C
- Normal Vibration Range: 0.1 to 2.2 mm/s
- Normal Current Draw: 12A to 22A
- Cooling System: Water-cooled mold circuit

TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE & FAILURE CODES:
- CODE I401: Heater Zone Failure
  - Cause: Burnt heater band, thermocouple failure, or SSR relay fault.
  - Action: Test heater band resistance, inspect thermocouple, replace SSR if needed.

- CODE I402: Hydraulic Oil Overheating
  - Cause: Cooling water blockage, dirty oil filter, pump overload, or low oil level.
  - Action: Check cooling water flow, replace oil filter, inspect pump, refill oil.

- CODE I403: Mold Clamping Error
  - Cause: Clamp sensor fault, misaligned mold, hydraulic pressure loss, or tie-bar wear.
  - Action: Check clamp sensor, realign mold, verify hydraulic pressure.

- CODE I404: Injection Pressure Instability
  - Cause: Worn screw tip, material contamination, nozzle blockage, or pressure sensor fault.
  - Action: Clean nozzle, inspect screw tip, verify material quality, test pressure sensor.

MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE:
- Hydraulic Oil Filter: Replace every 1000 operating hours.
- Heater Bands: Inspect every 500 operating hours.
- Mold Cooling Channels: Flush every 300 operating hours.
- Screw and Barrel: Inspect every 1500 operating hours.
- Clamp Mechanism: Lubricate every 250 operating hours.

SAFETY NOTES:
- Barrel and nozzle remain hot after shutdown.
- Release hydraulic pressure before servicing clamp or injection unit.
- Do not bypass mold safety gate interlocks.
