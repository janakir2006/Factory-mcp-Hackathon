HYDRAULIC PRESS MODEL HP-800 MAINTENANCE MANUAL

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model: HP-800-HeavyPress
- Machine Type: Hydraulic Press
- Press Capacity: 800 tons
- Hydraulic Pump Motor: 30kW
- Normal Hydraulic Oil Temperature Range: 30°C to 55°C
- Maximum Allowable Oil Temperature: 65°C
- Normal Vibration Range: 0.1 to 2.0 mm/s
- Normal Current Draw: 20A to 35A
- Operating Pressure Range: 120 bar to 250 bar

TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE & FAILURE CODES:
- CODE H501: Hydraulic Oil Overheating
  - Cause: Oil cooler blockage, low oil level, pump wear, or excessive pressure load.
  - Action: Clean oil cooler, refill oil, inspect pump, verify pressure settings.

- CODE H502: Pressure Drop
  - Cause: Internal leakage, worn seals, valve malfunction, or pump degradation.
  - Action: Inspect seals, test control valves, check pump output pressure.

- CODE H503: Ram Movement Irregular
  - Cause: Guide rail friction, air in hydraulic line, contaminated oil, or valve sticking.
  - Action: Bleed hydraulic system, replace oil filter, inspect guide rails, test valve.

- CODE H504: Excessive Pump Vibration
  - Cause: Cavitation, misalignment, loose mounting, or worn pump bearing.
  - Action: Check suction line, tighten mounting bolts, inspect coupling alignment.

MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE:
- Hydraulic Oil: Replace every 2000 operating hours.
- Oil Filter: Replace every 500 operating hours.
- Seal Inspection: Every 750 operating hours.
- Pressure Valve Calibration: Every 1000 operating hours.
- Guide Rail Lubrication: Every 250 operating hours.

SAFETY NOTES:
- Release hydraulic pressure before maintenance.
- Never work under the ram without mechanical safety blocks.
- Use lock-out/tag-out before servicing hydraulic pump or electrical panel.
